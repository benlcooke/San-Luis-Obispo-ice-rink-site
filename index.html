How Community Support Helps Rinks Get Built

In many California communities, rinks become real when early supporters create visible momentum ‚Äî proving demand, attracting sponsors, and strengthening the case for larger gifts and public/private partnerships.

Step 1: Visible community demand
Donations, shares, sign-ups, and turnout show decision-makers that the region truly wants the facility.
Step 2: Sponsors + anchor donors
As momentum grows, sponsors and major donors are more likely to commit.
Step 3: Partnership + build phase
With demand and funding traction, site selection, design, permitting, and construction become actionable.
