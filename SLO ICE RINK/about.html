Where We’re At

A feasibility study has been completed with Cal Poly, sponsor interest exists, potential parcels with appropriate zoning have been evaluated, and the project has been presented to SLO City Council with encouragement to grow community momentum.

Validated demand
We’ve taken early steps to confirm community need and long-term viability.
Real site work
We’ve evaluated multiple potential parcels that could support an ice + events facility.
Momentum matters
Visible support helps unlock larger sponsorships, major gifts, and partnership steps.

Why Community Momentum Matters

Many successful rink projects accelerate once community demand becomes visible—making sponsorships, major gifts, and partnership steps easier to secure.

Proof of demand
Donations, shares, sign-ups, and turnout show decision-makers the region truly wants the facility.
Lower perceived risk
Sponsors commit faster when they see broad participation and excitement.
Better leverage
Momentum strengthens the case for public/private collaboration and long-term partnerships.
